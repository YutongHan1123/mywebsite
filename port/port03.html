This is a project Jinqi, Zihao and I worked together during the Visual Discovery Conference. Jinqi created the website, Zihao added special effects, I did the process video.

The project is about the process of craft beer making.


It's a huge challenge for our group because we only had three days to finish. With such limited time, we collaborated with each other. I have to say, we have proper program design, detailed rules, clear division of different jobs, and material preparation. In this case, we successed.
